Privacy Policy

Unlocker respects your privacy and complies with all data protection regulations. Learn how we collect, store, and use data, and how you can control your privacy settings.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At CORS Unlocker, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how our browser extension operates and the important privacy considerations you should know about.

🔒 Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. All extension settings are stored locally on your device and never leave your browser.

How We Protect Your Privacy

📱 Local Storage Only

All configuration settings, website permissions, and preferences are stored exclusively in your browser’s local storage. No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

🔓 Open Source Transparency

Our extension is completely open source and available for review on GitHub. You can inspect every line of code to verify our privacy claims.

🚫 No Ads, No Tracking

CORS Unlocker is completely free and contains no advertisements, tracking scripts, or analytics. We’re committed to providing a clean, distraction-free experience.

Important Security Considerations

⚠️ Understanding Cross-Origin Risks

When you enable CORS Unlocker for a website, that site gains the ability to access resources from other domains. This powerful capability comes with important privacy and security implications:

🌐 Cross-Origin Access Enabled websites can fetch data from other domains that would normally be blocked by browser security policies.

🔑 Authentication Sharing With “Site Auth” enabled, websites can access your logged-in sessions and authentication data from other sites.

🛡️ Best Practices for Safe Usage

✅ Safe Practices

  • Only enable for websites you trust completely
  • Regularly review and clean up active permissions
  • Use “Site Auth” sparingly and only when necessary
  • Monitor extension activity in developer tools

❌ Avoid These Risks

  • Enabling for untrusted or suspicious websites
  • Using “Site Auth” on public or shared computers
  • Leaving permissions active for unused sites
  • Ignoring browser security warnings

Your Rights and Controls

🎛️ Complete Control

You maintain full control over which websites can access cross-origin resources. Every request for CORS access requires your explicit approval through our permission system.

🗑️ Easy Management

  • Revoke Access: Remove permissions for any website at any time through the extension options
  • Granular Control: Manage permissions individually for each domain
  • Real-time Monitoring: View active permissions and recent activity
